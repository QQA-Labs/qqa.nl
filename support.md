## FAQ

### My progress is gone

Check whether the app was offloaded/removed or storage cleanup tools are enabled.  
Email us your device + OS version and we'll help restore what we can.

### Premium / Restore purchases

If you purchased premium, use **Restore Purchases** in the app settings.  
If it still doesn't work, email **support@qqa.nl**

### Privacy

See our [Privacy Policy](privacy) for details on what data we collect and how we use it.
