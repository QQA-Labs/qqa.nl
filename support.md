# Support

For help, bugs, or feature requests:  
Email **support@qqa.nl**

## What to include (saves time)
- App name + version
- Device model
- OS version
- Steps to reproduce
- What you expected vs what happened
- Screenshot/screen recording if possible

## FAQ

### My progress is gone
Check whether the app was offloaded/removed or storage cleanup tools are enabled.  
Email us your device + OS version and we’ll help.

### Premium / Restore purchases
If you purchased premium, use **Restore Purchases** in the app settings.  
If it still doesn’t work, email support.ort@qqa.nl
